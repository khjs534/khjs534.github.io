Necropolis

A Diabolical Dungeon Crawler

Necropolis is a game of combat and survival, set in a magical labyrinth full of traps and swarms of monsters. There are several levels to the dungeon and they are partly randomly generated. You'll see similar things on particular levels but they are randomly generated to that theme.

The game is up to four players and scales accordingly. It's a great game to paly with friends and even better if you're all on a discord channel or teamspeak. There are great elements of the game that really make you feel like you are delving into the dungeon.

Here are some facts about Necropolis:

  • Necropolis is made by a relatively small studio, Harebrained Schemes.
  • There are tons of unique weapons to find throughout your dungeon crawl
  • Ranged weapons are OP
